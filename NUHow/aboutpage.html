About NUHow

"A Guide For Nationalians, by Nationalians."

What is it?


  NUHow is a platform designed specifically for National University Philippines students. Feeling lost navigating campus resources? Unsure about an upcoming event or class schedule? Or have a specific subject question that needs clarification? NUHow is here to help!

  This student-to-student platform allows you to ask any inquiry related to your university life. Whether it's about university offices, events, classes, or specific subjects, NUHow helps you connect with fellow NU students who can share their knowledge and experiences.


Our Goals


  At NUHow, we're passionate about empowering NU students to navigate their university experience with confidence. Here's what we strive for:

  • Empowering Student-to-Student Support: We aim to foster a supportive online community where NU students can rely on their peers for accurate and helpful information.
  • Centralized Resource Hub: We want NUHow to be your go-to platform for all things NU-related. Whether it's finding office hours, understanding class requirements, or discovering upcoming events, NUHow should be your first stop.
  • Building a Collaborative Network: We envision NUHow as a platform that fosters a sense of community and collaboration among NU students. By sharing knowledge and experiences, we aim to make navigating university life a smoother experience for everyone.

Post Guidelines


  To keep our platform informative, helpful, and respectful, here are some quick guidelines to follow when creating a post:

  • Keep it NU-related: Make sure your question or information is directly connected to National University Philippines.
  • Be clear and concise: Frame your question or information in a way that's easy for others to understand.
  • Search before you post: Check if your question has already been asked and answered on NUHow. Utilize the search bar to avoid redundancy.
  • Be respectful: NUHow is a platform for students to help students. Maintain a respectful tone in your posts and avoid personal attacks, insults, or offensive language.
  • Stay on topic: Avoid going off on tangents or introducing irrelevant topics to your post.
